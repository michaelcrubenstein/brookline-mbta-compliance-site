What is the town doing to comply with the MBTA-CA?

The town is proceeding in two ways:

  1. The Planning department is developing a zoning update for Harvard Street.
  2. The Select Board has charged the MBTA-CA Multifamily Permitting Committee with exploring other options.

The Planning Department's web page devoted to this project answers this question in detail.

What guidelines has the EOHLC published?

The EOHLC has published guidelines that describe how cities and towns must comply with the MBTA-CA.

For example, there are guidelines for allowing multi-family housing "as of right"

The following are examples of requirements that would be deemed to be inconsistent with “as of right” use: …; and (iii) a requirement that multi-family use must be combined with commercial or other uses on the same lot or as part of a single project. Mixed use projects may be allowed as of right in a multi-family zoning district, as long as multi-family housing is separately allowed as of right.

How can we keep our commercial districts like Coolidge Corner viable and still be compliant with the MBTA-CA?

  1. Form-based zoning can help us comply with the MBTA-CA. By specifying form-based zoning by-laws, we can constrain the buildings that are multi-family as of right to dramatically favor the maintenance and creation of first-floor retail. For example, making the first floor at ground level and have its frontage be 80% transparent.
  2. The Economic Development Advisory Board and the Housing Advisory Board are working on a proposal to provide developer relief for commercial retail.
  3. By increasing the residential population that is proximate to Coolidge Corner, we can better support the commercial activity there.
